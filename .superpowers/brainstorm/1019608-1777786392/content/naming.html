Recommendation

Stick with "OpenCode Harness"

"Harness" is the strongest metaphor for what this extension does — it's not just a wrapper or viewer, it channels and directs opencode's power through the IDE. It implies control, integration, and amplification.

"Cockpit" implies a dashboard (too passive). "Bridge" is too generic. "Lens" implies observation only (no control). "Pilot" is decent but less distinctive.
